Delicious Lemonad

Recipe by:Lizbeth

Ingredients

  • 5 lemons
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar

Equipment

  • measuring cup
  • jar
  • lemon squeezer
  • large spoon

Directions

  • 1. Pour 5 cups of water into jar
  • 2. Add 1 cup of sugar and mix until fully disolved
  • 3. Squeeze 5 lemons into jar and mix
  • 4. Add ice if desired and serve!
